About Me

I am Abdollah Zakeri, currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Houston, where I specialize in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision. My academic journey began in Iran at the University of Birjand and continued at Shahrood University of Technology, laying a strong foundation for my research. My passion for technology has led me to work on innovative projects, from biometric authentication to AI-driven healthcare solutions. I have developed multi-modal classification systems, advanced object detection models, and conducted pioneering research in pediatric COVID-19 severity detection using Chest X-Ray imaging. As a teacher, I have shared my knowledge in Advanced Programming, Data Structures, and AI, nurturing the next generation of tech enthusiasts. My work has been recognized in various publications, and I am constantly seeking new opportunities to innovate and contribute to the field of AI and Computer Vision.
