Abdollah Zakeri

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Houston, specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision. My research interests include computer vision and image/video processing, natural language processing and LLMs, multi-modal models and LMMs, and applications of computer vision in robotics.
